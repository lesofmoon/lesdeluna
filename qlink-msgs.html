QLink - Messaging Feature

A messaging feature in the Quipper LMS platform designed for teachers and students. Its purpose is to enable teachers to send notifications to class group chats regarding assignments, upcoming exams, advisories, and more. It also serves as a way for students to communicate with their classes when they are away from school.

Led the initiative to enhance user requirements by conducting user research, visiting schools across the Philippines, including provinces in Luzon and Mindanao. Gathered insights from research and implemented them through wireframes, high-fidelity designs, and the development and launch of the feature.
