QLearn App - Essay Writer Feature

An additional feature in the Quipper LMS platform designed for teachers to assign essay-writing tasks to their students. Students can write their essays directly online, while teachers can set scoring criteria, use a spell checker, upload images, track the word count, and more.

Led the initiative to enhance user requirements by conducting user testing with prototypes and visiting schools across the Philippines, including provinces in Luzon and Mindanao. Gathered insights from research and implemented them through wireframes, high-fidelity designs, and the development and launch of the feature.
